Who We Are

The Giving Story, also known as G-Story, is a youth leadership service club. We are NextGen Korean-Americans working together to strengthen our community through service, compassion, and leadership.

We are a network of passionate volunteers dedicated to help the community. Through teamwork, advocacy, and direct community action, we work everyday to make the world a better place. Whether we are cleaning up trash or helping senior citizens, we are defined by our shared belief that ordinary people can do extraordinary things when they work together.
