Who We Are

The Giving Story, also known as G-Story, is a youth leadership service club. We are NextGen Korean-Americans working together to strengthen our community through service, compassion, and leadership.

What We Do

We serve our community through beautification, civic engagement, senior service, youth mentoring, education, charity, and compassion.

Our programs include Buds Academy, music, art, sports, language classes, homework help, coding, clothing donations, and service for people in need.
